Hand-drawn Kaleidoscope

Right Hand

  • Pinch your thumb and index figner together on your right hand to draw.

Left Hand

  • Use the left index finger to select the color.
  • Pinch and adjust the gap between your thumb and index finger to change the line weight. Flip to the back of your hand to confirm the selection.

Other function

  • Slider scale give a more precise adjustment to line weight.
  • Reset button is to clean the canvas, and the save button save a copy of the image to your computer.
